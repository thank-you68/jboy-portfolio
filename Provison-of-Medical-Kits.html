At De La Salle John Bosco College, safety and well-being remain at the heart of our mission. As part of our commitment to fostering a secure and caring Lasallian environment, the Office of the Environment, Health and Safety has successfully provided Medical Kits to every unit and department of De La Salle John Bosco College.

This initiative is more than the distribution of essential medical supplies, it is a proactive step toward building a culture of preparedness, responsiveness, and shared responsibility. By equipping our offices and departments with readily accessible first-aid resources, we empower every Lasallian to respond with care during emergencies and uphold the value of being our brothers' and sisters' keepers.

Together, let us continue to create a campus where compassion meets preparedness and where every member of our community feels safe, cared for, and empowered.
